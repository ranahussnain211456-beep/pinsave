📖 Step-by-Step Guide

How to Download
Pinterest Videos

Follow these simple steps on any device — iPhone, Android, PC, or Mac.

Step 1

Find the Pinterest Video

Open the Pinterest app or website and browse to find the video you want to download. Look for pins that have a play button — these are video pins.

💡 Tip: You can search keywords in Pinterest's search bar to quickly find specific videos.
Step 2

Copy the Pin URL

Tap or click on the video pin to open it. Then copy the URL:

  • On Mobile: Tap the Share icon → tap Copy Link.
  • On Desktop: Right-click the pin → Copy link address, or copy from your browser's address bar.
💡 Tip: The URL should look like: https://www.pinterest.com/pin/123456789/
Step 3

Paste the URL into PinSave

Come back to PinSave and paste the copied URL into the download box at the top of the homepage. You can use Ctrl+V on PC or long-press → Paste on mobile.

Step 4

Click "Download"

Press the red Download button. PinSave will fetch the video from Pinterest and prepare it for saving. This usually takes only a second or two.

Step 5

Save the Video to Your Device

Once the video is ready, a Save Video button will appear. Click it to download the video directly to your device.

  • iPhone/Safari: Tap and hold the video → Download Linked File.
  • Android/Chrome: Tap Save Video — it goes to your Downloads folder.
  • Desktop: The file will download automatically to your Downloads folder.
✅ Done! Your Pinterest video is now saved in HD quality, watermark-free.
